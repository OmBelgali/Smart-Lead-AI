How It Works

SmartLead AI uses machine learning to analyze lead details such as company, industry, funding, and engagement metrics. It predicts lead quality so your sales team can focus on the most promising leads first. Confidence bars show how certain the AI is about each lead quality category, making it easy to interpret at a glance.
